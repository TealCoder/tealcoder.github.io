About Inspiration Tree

The Site
Inspiration tree is an experiment in structuring the inspiring things you do and dreams you have. Arranged in a tree, we can watch the feeling of inspiration spread and intertwine through many people's increasingly-exciting lives.
 
If it isn't clear: This sight is a prototype under construction; feel free to play with it or tell me about ideas it gives you.
 
The Ohia Tree
We chose the Ohia tree to represent how people inspire one another; the ohia tree is the first plant to colonize barren lava flows and break new ground to for other tropical plants to follow. The tree / bush comes in all sorts of fun sizes and shapes just like the exciting things you do.
